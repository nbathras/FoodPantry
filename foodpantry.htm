FoodPantry is a mobile application that connects donors such as individuals, restaurants, and organizations to distributors, such as local food banks and food pantries, in order to specify which food and/or ingredients one is willing to donate. Users will be identified as either donors or distributors and will have varying app functionality depending on their role.
